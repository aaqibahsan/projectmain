Sinharaja Forest The Sinharaja Forest, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Sri Lanka, is renowned for its biodiversity hotspot status. Panthera pardus kotiya(Sri Lankan Leopard)
Udawalawe National Park Udawalawe National Park is renowned for its significant population of Sri Lankan elephants and diverse bird species. Grey-headed Fish Eagle (Ichthyophaga ichthyaetus)
Minneriya National Park The park is famous for hosting the Minneriya Elephant Gathering, where a large number of elephants gather around the Minneriya Tank during the dry season. This spectacle is considered one of the largest gatherings of wild elephants in the world. Elephant Gathering
